Letter from J. Fred Buzhardt to Sam J. Ervin Jr. (July 16 1973)

[Letter from J. Fred Buzhardt, Special Counsel to the President, to Sam J. Ervin, Jr., Chairman, Senate Select Committee on Presidential Campaign Activities, 9 Weekly Comp. Pres. Doc.905 (Jul. 16, 1973).]

[PDF | Master List]

Dear Mr. Chairman:

This letter is to confirm the fact, stated to your Committee today by Mr. Alexander Butterfield, that the President's meetings and conversations in the White House have been recorded since the spring of 1971.

I am advised that this system, which is still in use, is similar to that employed by the last Administration and which had been discontinued from 1969 until the spring of 1971.

A more detailed statement concerning these procedures will be furnished to the Committee shortly.

Sincerely,
J. Fred Buzhardt
