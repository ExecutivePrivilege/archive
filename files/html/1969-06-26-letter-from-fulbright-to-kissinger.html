U.S. SENATE,
COMMITTEE ON FOREIGN RELATIONS,
June 26, 1969.

Hon. HENRY A. KISSINGER,
Assistant to the President for National Security Affairs, The White House.

Dear DR. KISSINGER:

I wonder if you would be willing to meet with the Committee on Foreign Relations informally and in executive session for a general review of major foreign policy questions. I think that such a meeting would be most beneficial to the Committee, for we would then have a much clearer idea of the new Administrationâ€™s objectives and intentions.

Sincerely yours,
J. W. FULBRIGHT, Chairman.
