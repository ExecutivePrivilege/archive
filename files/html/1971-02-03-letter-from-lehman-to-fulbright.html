THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, February 3, 1971.

Hon. J. W. FULBRIGHT,
U.S. Senate,
Washington, D.C.

DEAR MR. CHAIRMAN: I have your letter of 2nd February requesting my appearance before the Committee on Foreign Relations.

I must inform you that I shall be unable to appear because of my position on the National Security Council Staff.

Yours,
J. F. LEHMAN, JR.,
Staff Member, National Security Council.
